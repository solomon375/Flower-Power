Petunias

General Information

Petunias are colorful, trumpet-shaped annuals available in many colors. They bloom throughout spring and summer.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun to partial shade.

Soil: Light, well-drained, slightly acidic.

Climate: Prefers warm weather, sensitive to cold.

Home Care & Tips

  • Water often but keep soil slightly dry between watering.
  • Fertilize weekly for constant flowers.
  • Trim leggy stems every few weeks.

Recommended Home Locations

  • Hanging baskets.
  • Window boxes.
  • Balcony rail planters.
  • Sunny patios.
  • Garden borders.
  • Indoors only with VERY strong sunlight.
