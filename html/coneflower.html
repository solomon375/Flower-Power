Coneflowers

General Information

Coneflowers (Echinacea) are hardy perennials known for their daisy-like shape and raised centers.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun.

Soil: Well-drained, poor to average soil.

Climate: Very hardyâ€”heat and drought tolerant.

Home Care & Tips

  • Water weekly until established, then minimal care needed.
  • Cut back in fall to encourage new growth next year.
  • Attracts bees, butterflies, and birds.

Recommended Home Locations

  • Sunny garden beds.
  • Pollinator gardens.
  • Outdoor pots on balconies.
  • Not good indoors (need full sun).
