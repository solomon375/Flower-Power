Flowerpedia About us

Flower Power runs entirely on volunteer passion and community support. We provide all our plant care guides, research, and resources completely free because we believe everyone should have access to reliable flower knowledge. To keep our website running, update our content, and expand our plant library, we depend on donations from plant lovers like you. Every contribution—big or small—helps us continue sharing accurate, beginner-friendly information with the world. If Flower Power has helped you grow, please consider helping us grow too.
