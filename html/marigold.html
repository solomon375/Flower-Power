Marigolds

General Information

Marigolds are bright, cheerful annual flowers known for their orange and yellow colors. They are easy to grow and naturally repel pests.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun.

Soil: Well-drained, moderately fertile.

Climate: Warm climates, tolerate heat well.

Home Care & Tips

  • Water regularly but avoid overwatering.
  • Deadhead to encourage constant blooming.
  • Great for gardens as natural pest repellents.

Recommended Home Locations

  • Sunny balconies.
  • Outdoor garden beds.
  • Raised planters.
  • Porch steps or entryway pots.
  • Not ideal for indoors (need strong sun).
