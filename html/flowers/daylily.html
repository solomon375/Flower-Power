Daylilies

General Information

Daylilies are hardy, low-maintenance perennials that produce trumpet-shaped flowers. Each bloom lasts one day, but the plant produces many buds.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun or partial shade.

Soil: Any well-drained soil.

Climate: Extremely adaptable.
