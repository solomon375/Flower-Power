Hollyhocks

General Information

Hollyhocks are tall biennial/perennial flowers with large, colorful blooms that grow on long stalks.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun.

Soil: Rich, well-drained.

Climate: Warm to temperate regions.

Home Care & Tips

  • Water regularly, especially in dry weather.
  • Support tall stalks with stakes.
  • Remove infected leaves to prevent rust disease.

Recommended Home Locations

  • Along walls, fences, or sheds.
  • Large garden beds.
  • Back row of flower borders.
  • Not for indoor growing (very tall).
