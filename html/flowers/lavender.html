Lavender

General Information

Lavender is a fragrant perennial known for its purple blooms and strong scent. Used in oils, perfumes, and gardens.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun.

Soil: Dry, sandy, well-drained.

Climate: Hot, dry Mediterranean-type climates.
