Sunflowers

General Information

Sunflowers are tall, iconic plants known for turning toward the sun. They produce large, bright yellow blooms.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun.

Soil: Loose, fertile soil.

Climate: Warm, dry climates.

Home Care & Tips

  • Water deeply once per week.
  • Provide support for tall varieties.
  • Great for pollinators (bees, birds).

Recommended Home Locations

  • Backyard garden beds.
  • Large outdoor pots.
  • Sunny balconies (dwarf varieties only).
  • Along fences or walls.
  • Not suitable indoors.
