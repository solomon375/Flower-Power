Canterbury Bells

General Information

Canterbury Bells are biennial flowers known for bell-shaped blooms in purple, blue, white, and pink.

Where They Grow

Sun: Full sun or partial shade.

Soil: Moist, fertile, well-drained.

Climate: Cool to temperate areas.
