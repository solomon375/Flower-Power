About Flower Power

Flower Power is a non profit made by volunteers. We search and share simple, true facts about many flowers and plants. We study how they grow and how to care for them. Our website is the home for this knowledge. We want to help beginners and plant lovers learn in an easy way. We depend on donations and new helpers to keep going.
