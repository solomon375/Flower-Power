About Flower Power

Sharing the beauty, science and stories behind every blossom.

Our Mission

Flower Power is a friendly nonâ€‘profit run by volunteers. We collect and test clear, basic knowledge about many flowers and plants. We make it easy for any person to understand: how a plant grows, what soil it likes, how much water and light, and common problems.

We do simple research and write guides in beginner English. We check facts before we publish. We keep updating pages when seasons change or new care tips are proven. Our goal: make flower knowledge delightful and accessible so beginners and enthusiasts can connect with nature, one petal at a time.

All our work is powered by volunteers. We welcome new helpers who like observing plants, writing simple explanations, taking clear photos, or reviewing care steps. Donations help us pay hosting costs and tools so we can continue sharing accurate information freely.

If you want to join: read a plant, record what you see, share results, and help us improve every guide. Together we build a trusted home for flower learning online.
